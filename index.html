About

I am an NIHR Clinical Lecturer at the University of Bristol, focusing on outcomes from severe infection, differentiating phenotypes of infection, and molecular approaches to diagnosis. My research combines clinical work, genomics, and causal inference from observational data to improve patient outcomes.
