About Me

I am an Academic Clinical Lecturer in Infectious Diseases at the University of Bristol, with a focus on COVID-19 research, sepsis, and host responses to infection. My work combines clinical expertise with genomic approaches to understand and improve patient outcomes.

My research has contributed significantly to understanding COVID-19 treatments and outcomes, including work on the RECOVERY trial and studies of long-term COVID-19 effects. I am particularly interested in understanding the mechanisms of host response to infection and developing new approaches to improve patient outcomes.

Research Interests

COVID-19 Research

Clinical trials and outcomes research, including the RECOVERY trial and long-term effects studies. My work has contributed to understanding treatment effectiveness and patient outcomes in COVID-19.

Genomics

Host responses to infection and genetic determinants of disease outcomes. Using genomic approaches to understand individual variation in response to infection.

Sepsis

Understanding mechanisms and improving outcomes in sepsis. Research focuses on early detection, treatment optimization, and long-term outcomes.
